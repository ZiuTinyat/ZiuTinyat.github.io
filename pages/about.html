My experiences

I'm a master student in Entertainment Technology Center, at Carnegie Mellon University.

Before ETC, I was a college student at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University studying Internet and Multimedia Technology. This is where I started programming and fell in love with game development. I learned C++, C#, Java, JS through various game projects, and gradually understood OOP and some design patterns through tons of practices. Besides, I had courses about linear algebra, signal processing, computer vision, database and so on. In my senior year, I went to GGJ Hong Kong 2017 at the first time and won the Best Game Integration Award with the game "Ripple Blossom", which encouraged me a lot.
